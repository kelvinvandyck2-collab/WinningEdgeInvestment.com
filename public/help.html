Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
To create an account, you'll need a registration token from an existing member. Once you have a token, click on the Register button in the top navigation, enter your token, and follow the registration process.
What is the minimum investment?
The minimum investment amount varies by package. Our Starter package has a minimum of $500, Professional requires $2,000, and Premium starts at $5,000.
How often are profits distributed?
Profits are distributed monthly on the same day you made your initial investment. You can choose to withdraw your profits or reinvest them for compound growth.
What cryptocurrencies do you accept?
We currently accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), and USDT (ERC-20 and TRC-20). More cryptocurrencies will be added in the future.
How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawals are typically processed within 24 hours. During high volume periods, it may take up to 48 hours. Crypto withdrawals depend on network congestion.
